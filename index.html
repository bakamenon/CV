🤟Vijay Menon - The Urban Nomad🤟

Aspiring Writer, Photographer and Amatuer chef - Also a Senior Product Manager at an automotive company.

He's an Indian who is married to a Chinese and currently living in Japan... Because, the world is getting smaller 😝.

Vijay moved to Japan from China last year (actually end of December 2018) to work for Nissan in the Yokohama Global Headquarters. Vijay works as a Senior Product Manager in the Sales systems.

Vijay's parents are originally from Kannur, though he grew up in Aluva and Bangalore. He is an engineer by training and profession, but passionate about writing. He has a self published a book on Amazon with 5 five ⭐ ratings 😝, and the book is called Shanghai - A Year To Remember . He also loves cooking, photography and long meandering walks.

Vijay moved to China back in 2008 for 6 months and then ended up staying for just over ten years. In 2015 he met Gina who would become his wife in 2018, just before they left China. Gina is Chinese, who loves Onam and Vishu celebrations, and Bangalore as much as Vijay does. And yes... Vijay's wife is the guinea pig for his cooking experiments 😄.
